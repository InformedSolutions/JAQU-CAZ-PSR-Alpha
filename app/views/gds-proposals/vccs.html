If your vehicle shows a daily charge, you do not need to pay it until the zones are live.

The zones will operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, every day of the year.

How we calculate your charge
We use this information when calculating your charge:

  • vehicle type
  • emission standards
  • Clean Air Zone (CAZ)
For example:
A private car (Euro 4) in Birmingham (CAZ D) = £8.00
A private car (Euro 4) in Leeds (CAZ B) = £0.00 (No Charge)

There are 4 categories of Clean Air Zone; each one charges different types of vehicle. For example, Birmingham charges private cars (CAZ D) but Leeds does not (CAZ B).

Where are Clean Air Zones

There are currently three Charging Clean Air Zones. These are Bath, Birmingham and Portsmouth. London also has a similar scheme called the Ultra Low Emission Zone.

Birmingham

Birmingham's Clean Air Zone will cover all roads within the A4540 Middleway Ring Road but not the Middleway itself. This includes the A38 and tunnels. More information on Birmingham's boundary.
