Check if you'll be charged to drive in a Clean Air Zone

Use this service to find out if there will be a daily charge to drive your vehicle in a Clean Air Zone.

You might need to pay to drive in a Clean Air Zone if your vehicle does not meet emissions standards.

Bath, Birmingham and Leeds will have the first Clean Air Zones from early 2021.

If you want to drive in London you should check if you need to pay to drive in the Ultra Low Emission Zone on the Transport for London (TfL) website.
