Exemptions

There are some national and local exemptions from the charge

You are automatically entitles to a nation exemption, and do not have to pay a charge, if you have a:

  • vehicle that's ultra low emission
  • disabled passenger tax class vehicle
  • military vehicle
  • historic vehicle
  • vehicle retrofitted with technology accredited by the Clean Air Vehicle Retrofit Accreditation Scheme (CVRAS)

Local exemptions are managed by the local authority area you're driving in.

  • Bath
  • Birmingham
  • Leeds
