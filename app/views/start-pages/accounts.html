Accounts for multiple vehicles

If you own or manage multiple vehicles creating an account is the easiest way to check and pay charges. It's free and allows you to tell us which vehicles drive in a clean air zone.

You’ll need:

  • a file (CSV) of all your number plates (or you can add the vehicles individually)
  • which town or city you’re driving your vehicles in
  • a debit or credit card
