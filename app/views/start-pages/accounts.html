Business account for multiple vehicles

If you own or manage more than 2 vehicles creating an account is the easiest way to check and pay charges. It's free and allows you to tell us which vehicles drive in a clean air zone.

You’ll need:

  • your company name
  • a file (CSV) of all your number plates (or you can add the vehicles individually)
  • which town or city you’re driving your vehicles in
  • a debit or credit card
