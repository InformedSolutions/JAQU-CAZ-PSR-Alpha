Business account for multiple vehicles

If you own or manage 2 or more vehicles, creating an account is the easiest way to check and pay charges.
This service works by you telling us which vehicles have driven in a zone. Your number plates determine if your vehicles meet emissions standards in the Clean Air Zones. If they don't, you can see what the charges are and pay them.

Payment must be made a maximum of 7 days after driving in the zone or you may receive a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN).

To create an account you'll need:

  • your company name
  • an email address
Create an account

Once your account is set up you can:

  • upload all your vehicle number plates by file (CSV) or add them one by one
  • manage your vehicles - add and remove
  • view what each vehicle will be charged in every Clean Air Zone
  • pay your charges by credit/debit card or Direct Debit
