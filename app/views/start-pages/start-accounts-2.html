Drive in a Clean Air Zone - Check and pay for multiple vehicles

Business accounts for multiple vehicles

If you own or manage 2 or more vehicles, creating an account is the easiest way to check how much you will be charged.

You will need to upload your vehicle number plates to this service. Your number plates will be used to determine whether your vehicles meet the emission standards in each zone. If they don't, you can check what your charges will be.

Once your account is set up you can:

  • upload all your vehicle number plates by file (CSV) or add them one by one
  • manage your vehicles - add and remove
  • view what each vehicle will be charged in every Clean Air Zone

To create an account you'll need:

  • your company name
  • an email address
