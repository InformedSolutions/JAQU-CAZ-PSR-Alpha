Drive in a Clean Air Zone

Use this service to find out if there will be a daily charge to drive your vehicle in a Clean Air Zone and if there is pay the charge.

You might need to pay to drive in a Clean Air Zone if your vehicle does not meet emissions standards.

The first Clean Air Zone launched in Bath on 15 March 2021.

The next Clean Air Zone will launch in Birmingham on 1 June 2021.

If you want to drive in London you should check if you need to pay to drive in the Ultra Low Emission Zone on the Transport for London (TfL) website

This service cannot check vehicles that are registered outside the UK.

Before you start

Checking and paying for multiple vehicles

If you’re a business and want to check and pay for multiple vehicles a you’ll need:

  • an account - you can create one when you use this service
  • your company name
  • an email address
  • a credit/debit card or bank details
  • a spreadsheet in CSV format of the number plates (registration numbers) of the vehicles you want to check
