Check if you'll be charged to drive in a Clean Air Zone

Use this service to find out if there will be a daily charge to drive your vehicle in a Clean Air Zone.

You might need to pay to drive in a Clean Air Zone if your vehicle does not meet emissions standards.

The first Clean Air Zone launched in:

  • Bath on 15 March 2021

The next Clean Air Zone will launch in:

  • Birmingham on 1 June 2021
If you want to drive in London you should check if you need to pay to drive in the Ultra Low Emission Zone on the Transport for London (TfL) website

Before you start

Checking and paying for a single vehicle

You’ll need:

  • the number plate (registration number) of the vehicle you want to drive
  • to know which Clean Air Zone you’re driving the vehicle in
  • a credit/debit card

Checking and paying for multiple vehicles

You'll need:

  • to create an account or sign in
  • a spreadsheet (CSV) of the number plates (registration numbers) of the vehicles you want to check
  • your company name
  • an email address
  • a credit/debit card or Direct Debit
