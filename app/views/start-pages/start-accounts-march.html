Drive in a Clean Air Zone

When you need to pay Clean Air Zone charges:

  • Bath - 15 March 2021
  • Birmingham - 1 June 2021
If you want to drive in London you should check if you need to pay to drive in the Ultra Low Emission Zone on the Transport for London (TfL) website

This service cannot check vehicles that are registered outside the UK.

Start

Before you start

You’ll need the number plate (registration number) of the vehicle you want to drive.

Paying for a single vehicle

You’ll also need a credit or debit card.

Checking and paying for multiple vehicles

Businesses who want to check and pay for multiple vehicles will need:

  • an account - you can create one when you use this service
  • your company name
  • an email address
  • a credit/debit card or bank details
  • a spreadsheet (CSV) of the number plates (registration numbers) of the vehicles you want to check

All major credit and debit cards are accepted.

Contact Clean Air Zone Support

You can contact Clean Air Zone support if you cannot use the online service or you need help.
