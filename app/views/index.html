Pay a Clean Air Zone charge

You can use this service to pay a daily charge for driving in a Clean Air Zone if your vehicle does not meet Clean Air Standards.

Before you start, you'll need the registration details (number plate) of the vehicle you want to pay for.

Payment must be made a maximum of 7 days after you have driven in the zone or you will receive a penalty charge notice (PCN).
