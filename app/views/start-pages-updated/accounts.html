Check and pay the charge for multiple vehicles

If you own or manage 2 or more vehicles, creating an account is the easiest way to check and pay charges.

This service works by you telling us the number of days your vehicles drive in a Clean Air Zone. The number plates determine if the vehicles meet emissions standards in a zone. If they don't, you can see what the charges are and pay them.

To create an account you'll need:

  • your company name
  • an email address
Create an account

Once your account is set up you can:

  • upload all your vehicle number plates by file (CSV) or add them one by one
  • manage your vehicles - add and remove
  • add users to help manage the account
  • view what each vehicle will be charged in every Clean Air Zone
  • pay your charges by credit/debit card or Direct Debit (one-off payment)

Sign in to your account

! Warning Payment must be made a maximum of 7 days after you have driven in the zone or you may receive a penalty charge notice (PCN).
