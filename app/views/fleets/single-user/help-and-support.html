Exemptions

There are some national and local exemptions from the charge.

National

Vehicles that do not have to pay the charge as they are automatically entitled to a national exemption:

  • vehicle thatâ€™s ultra low emission
  • disabled passenger tax class vehicle
  • military vehicle
  • historic vehicle
  • vehicle retrofitted with technology accredited by the Clean Vehicle Retrofit Accreditation Scheme (opens in a new window) (CVRAS)

Local

Local exemptions are specific to the local authority of each CAZ and are managed by them. You usually have to apply for local exemptions.
