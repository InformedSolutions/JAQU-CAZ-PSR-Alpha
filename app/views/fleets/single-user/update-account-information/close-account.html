Closing an account

You should only close your account if you are sure that you will not need it again. Once closed it will no longer be accessible by you or anyone else with a user account.

You can set up another account in the same name, but you won't be able to access the payment history from this account.

To ensure you're not leaving anything important behind you might need later:

  • download the payment history
  • cancel any Direct Debits you have set up for Clean Air Zone charges
  • read our Privacy Notice to see what we do with your data
