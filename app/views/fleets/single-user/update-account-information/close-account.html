Closing an account

You should only close the account if you are sure the company will no longer need it. You and any team members who help manage the account will no longer be able to sign in.

If you are closing the account because none of the vehicles received a charge, be aware they could be charged in upcoming zones.

Before you go remember to:

  • download payment history
  • cancel any payment agreements with your bank that have been set up for Clean Air Zone charges
  • read our Privacy Notice to see what we do with your data
