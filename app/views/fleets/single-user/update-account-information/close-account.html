What happens to account data

  • Active number plate(s) are retained in our database as long as your account remains active. We delete the history of your vehicle(s) after 12 months.
  • We keep payment transactions in our system for 7 years. These include a record of the number plate against which you made a payment.
  • Email data is stored for 7 days and then deleted
  • We will only retain IP addresses for 30 days
