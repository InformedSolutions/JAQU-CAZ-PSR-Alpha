Closing an account

You should only close your account if you are sure you will not need it again.

You will no longer be able to sign in using your current email address and password. This will also apply to any other people who use your account. You can set up a new account with the same email address and a new password.

If you are closing your account because none of the vehicles received a charge, be aware they could be charged in upcoming zones.

Before you go remember to:

  • download the payment history
  • cancel any Direct Debits you've set up with your bank for Clean Air Zone charges
  • read our Privacy Notice to see what we do with your data
