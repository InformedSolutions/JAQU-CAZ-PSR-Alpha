Set up an agreement to pay by bank account

What you need to know before setting up:

  • you need to set up a mandate with each zone
  • paying this way means you need to authorise a payment every time you want to pay a charge - the money will not automatically come out of your account
  • we'll send a receipt by email to whoever makes the payment
  • we'll also send an email to whoever sets up the agreement when a payment is made. To let them know money is leaving the bank account

Zones you haven't set up an agreement with and where the local authority supports this type of payment will appear here.
