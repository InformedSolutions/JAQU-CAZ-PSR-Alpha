Which vehicles and days are you paying for?

This list only contains vehicles that are subject to a charge in {{data['caz']}}.

You can pay for the past 6 days and the next 7 days, which includes today.

A daily charge period runs from midnight to midnight.

Why can't I see my date?
If the date you want to pay for is not displayed, it may be because:

  • The 7 day period you had to pay has ended.
    You may be subject to a penalty charge notice (PCN).

  • The date is too far in the future.
    You should check back here closer to the date you want.
