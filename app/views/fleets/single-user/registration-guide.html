Uploading multiple vehicles

You can upload multiple vehicles at once to allow you to pay for multiple vehicles and check whether they are subject to charge.

To get started, dowload the CSV template. You can open the file in a spreadsheet program.

You will need to enter the number plates (Vehicle Registration Number) of all your vehicles. Do not enter anything else into the CSV file.

Once you have entered all the number plates of your vehicles, save the file and upload it. The name of the file does not matter.

The upload may take a couple of minutes, when this has completed you will be able to see where they are subject to charge and pay for multiple vehicles at once.
